Statement of Objectives

This research will be focus on developing the Speakable Me: A Gamified Learning App for DMMMSU-MLUC Kindergarten Pupils to enhance the learning capability of the children through mobile technology using a gamified approach.

Purpose of the Study

Specifically, the study aims to address the following objectives:

  1. Identify the learning needs;
  2. To develop a system that would adopt the learning needs of pupils;
  3. Assess the level of usability of the proposed system along;
    • Efficiency,
    • Affect,
    • Helpfulness,
    • Control, and
    • Learnability
Findings

1. Speakable Me: A Gamified Learning App for DMMMSU-MLUC Kindergarten Pupils is developed following the Design Science Research as the software methodology. Voice Recognition is used in developing the game. The features included in the game are recognition of shapes, alphabets, colors, numbers and animals.

2. Speakable Me: A Gamified Learning App for DMMMSU-MLUC Kindergarten Pupils is built using free-form speech recognition in applying voice recognition, Photoshop 2016 for the icons and pictures is integrated in the game.

Conclusions

1. Utilizing the Design Science Research (DSR) as the software methodology of Speakable Me: A Gamified Learning App for DMMMSU-MLUC Kindergarten Pupils is proven to be applicable voice.

2. Free-form speech recognition in applying voice recognition, Photoshop 2016 for the icons and pictures and the existing audio recorder of the mobile device is well utilized.

3. The developed application is described as highly acceptable, therefore, it achieves the intended purpose and objective of the Speakable Me: A Gamified Learning App for DMMMSU-MLUC Kindergarten Pupils.

Recommendations

1. 2. Free-form speech recognition is an applicable framework in voice recognition techniques. Also, the existing audio recorder of the mobile phone can be integrated in the game application.

3. Based from the grand mean of Speakable Me: A Gamified Learning App for DMMMSU-MLUC Kindergarten Pupils, the researchers recommended that the game application may be used as an additional material in the learning experience of kindergarten.

4. Future researchers conducting the same or related researchers must make further improvements to enhance the features of the interactive learning application such as adding more subjects in the game.
